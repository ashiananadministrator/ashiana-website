Transparency in Redevelopment

In accordance with section 79A guidelines of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, our society management is dedicated to upholding the highest levels of transparency and democracy in our redevelopment process. Every decision, resolution, financial feasibility report, and clearance is posted publically for all society members and tenants to verify.

Legal Compliance

Fully aligned with NMMC guidelines and CIDCO NOC protocols.

Member Consent

All major decisions backed by Special General Body resolutions.
